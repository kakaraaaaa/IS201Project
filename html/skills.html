My Special Skills

speaking in 3 languages, playing tennis, cooking and being creative

Posted by Kara Lin on April 14, 2022

I have played tennis for my whole life. I have learned a ton of things from tennis that I don't think other people can ever perceive.

First, I learned to balance my life. Since I am a student-athlete, I am constantly very busy and exhausted. I learned to make a schedule and be discipline to my plans. I always make sure I eat healthy food and rest well. Therefore, one of my biggest skills is to learn how to cook healthy food for myself.

Second, I always face challenges in tennis, and they help me to become stronger as a tennis player as well as an individual. I learn to be tough and persistent no matter what.

One of my unique skills is I can speak in three languages. I am very fluent in Cantonese and Mandarin. English is my second language. I often help my family and friends translate messages.

I consider myself a creative person. I always have differnt ideas and thoughts in my mind. People always compliment me that I am a "thinker" because I love pointing out things.
